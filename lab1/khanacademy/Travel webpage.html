Travel to Almaty

Almaty is the southern capital of Kazakhstan. This is a very sunny, green city, which is comfortably located at the foot of the mountains of the Trans-Ili Alatau. Almaty is not called a garden city for nothing, half of it is occupied by green spaces — poplar, oak, birch, linden, acacia, pine and spruce. Almaty
